FREQUENTLY
ASKED QUESTIONS

01   Do I Need To Book, And
      Can I Reschedule

We advise all players to pre-book to avoid disappointment! You can book online via the website or call us. We understand that things come up and therefore, if you call us 24 hours before your session, we can reschedule the booking. For group bookings & parties we require 14 days’ notice to reschedule your booking.

02   Is There A Minimum Age
       Requirement

There is no minimum age to play! We have a range of club sizes for players of all ages. We do kindly ask that all players be able to safely hold and manoeuvre with our clubs in order to play.

03   CAN I Watch? How Long
       Does It Take Play?

To answer your question…yes you can watch. We get it, sometimes crazy golf just isn’t your thing! We request that anyone 12 and under is accompanied by an adult as well. To play both courses takes between 35-55mins however, on a busy day this can be slightly longer!

04   Do We Serve Food And
        Drinks?

Yes, we do. Crazy golf without a drink and treat is a no-no as far as we are concerned! You can order both food and drink at the café.
